The Sideline gives you quick sports pop-culture talking points before you walk into the room.

Built for people who do not follow sport but still have sporty people in their life: colleagues, partners, friends, kids, parents, and everyone at the party with a sudden opinion about a quarterback.

Pick the room you are walking into and read a short briefing:

- One plain-English TL;DR you can say out loud.
- 3 to 6 talking points framed for non-fans.
- Pop-culture angles, drama, nice-guy stories, and redemption arcs.
- A source link on every point.
- One question to ask a fan to keep the conversation going.

Not a scores app, betting app, fantasy app, or dense sports news feed. Just enough context to feel included.

Free: Cocktail Party context with a daily national briefing.

The Sideline Pro unlocks all contexts, fresher briefings up to 3 times a day, and Local Team personalisation.
