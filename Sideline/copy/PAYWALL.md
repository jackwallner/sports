# RevenueCat Paywall Input

Recommended template: feature-list.

The paywall should feel like a short utility upgrade, not a sports subscription. Use a compact hero, 3 benefits, monthly and annual products, and RevenueCat-provided pricing/trial tokens.

## Hero

Symbol direction: `quote.bubble.fill` over a sideline-like green capsule. No team, league, or athlete imagery.

Recommended headline: `Every room, covered`

Other headline candidates:

- `Know what to say`
- `More rooms, less homework`
- `Sound in the loop`

Subhead: `One room is free. Pro gives you all 4 contexts and fresher briefings.`

## Benefits

- `person.3` - `All 4 contexts`
- `clock.arrow.circlepath` - `Fresh 3× a day`
- `quote.bubble` - `Built for non-fans`

## Trial And Pricing

Trial line: `Start with {trial}. Cancel anytime.`

Pricing: show monthly and annual. Frame annual as savings with RevenueCat-provided pricing.

Restore Purchases, Terms, and Privacy must remain visible in the footer.
