What Gist Needs

Gist works without an account. It fetches cached briefings from our servers and uses RevenueCat only to check whether Gist Pro is active.

Troubleshooting

  • If a briefing looks stale, pull to refresh or wait for the next scheduled update (morning, midday, or evening for Pro).
  • If Pro features stay locked after purchase, open Settings and tap Restore Purchases.
  • If you are offline, the app shows the last successful briefing it cached on your device.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions are handled by Apple. To manage or cancel, open the App Store app, tap your Apple ID, then choose Subscriptions. You can also tap Manage Subscription in Gist Settings when Pro is active.

Privacy

Gist does not require an account and does not include ads or cross-app tracking.
