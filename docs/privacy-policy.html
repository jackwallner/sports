Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 16, 2026

Gist gives sports conversation briefings without accounts, social features, ads, or tracking.

Information We Collect

The app does not require an account and does not collect your name, email address, contacts, photos, precise location, or health data.

The app may contact Supabase to fetch the latest cached briefing for the selected context. Standard server logs may include technical information such as request time, IP address, user agent, and status code for security and reliability.

Purchases

Subscriptions are handled by RevenueCat and Apple. We use RevenueCat only to determine whether Gist Pro is active. We do not receive your full payment card details.

AI And Sources

Briefings are generated server-side from curated public sports sources and cached before the app fetches them. The app does not send your personal prompts to Gemini.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We use service providers needed to run the app: Supabase for hosting cached briefings, Google Gemini for server-side generation, RevenueCat for subscriptions, and Apple for App Store purchases.

Children

Gist is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
